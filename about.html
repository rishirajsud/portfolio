About Hi, I'm Rishi Sud

Building intelligent systems solving real business problems.

I’m on a mission to turn data into smarter decisions and AI into actionable results. With 9+ years of experience in AI, full-stack development, and cloud engineering, I combine technical expertise with creative problem-solving to build solutions that make a difference. My work is driven by curiosity, innovation, and the desire to help businesses reach new heights.

How I Got Here

My journey began with a passion for technology and problem-solving, earning a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Computer Science. Early in my career, I honed my skills as a software engineer at Capgemini, building robust applications and learning the foundations of full-stack development.
Over the years, I transitioned into AI and machine learning, working with organizations like Futurewei Technologies, Toyota Connected, and Cross Country Healthcare, where I built intelligent systems, optimized workflows, and scaled AI models to deliver measurable business impact.
Today, at Consumer Reports, I design and deploy AI-driven solutions that improve efficiency, accuracy, and scalability. My journey has been fueled by curiosity, continuous learning, and a drive to bridge cutting-edge technology with real-world business results.
