About

Building intelligent systems solving real business problems.

I’m Rishi Sud, a Senior Full Stack AI/ML Engineer with 9+ years of experience building intelligent systems that deliver measurable results. I specialize in turning complex data into actionable insights, automating workflows, and deploying scalable AI solutions that drive growth across industries.

When I’m not architecting AI pipelines or coding scalable solutions, I enjoy mentoring teams, exploring the latest AI innovations, and finding creative ways to solve problems.
